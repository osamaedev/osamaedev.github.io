Hashy - Android Application -

  1. Description

Hashy is an android application, that consist of two parts, a seller and buyer, where the buyer can make an order of different categories, once the order is confirmed a notification is sent the to near sellers to respond to this order. So the whole idea in this two apps is that only the seller can make an order, this order is sent to the nearest buyers, those buyers receive the complete information about the order, and also the distance between them and the buyer, and based on these information, they decide to respond or not to a particular order.
